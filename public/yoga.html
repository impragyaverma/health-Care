Yoga

Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)

Surya Namaskar is a sequence of 12 powerful yoga poses that offer a variety of health benefits. It is best performed early in the morning on an empty stomach.

Other Yoga Poses

1. Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Benefits: Stretches the whole body, strengthens the arms and legs, improves digestion.

2. Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Benefits: Improves balance and stability, strengthens legs and core muscles.

3. Warrior Pose II (Virabhadrasana II)

Benefits: Strengthens legs, opens hips, improves stamina and concentration.

4. Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

Benefits: Stretches chest and lungs, improves flexibility of spine, strengthens the arms.

5. Child's Pose (Balasana)

Benefits: Relieves back and neck pain, stretches hips, thighs, and ankles, calms the mind.

6. Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)

Benefits: Strengthens back, glutes, and hamstrings, improves digestion, rejuvenates tired legs.
